## Overview

The `/candidates` endpoint returns all election results for a specific candidate across every contest they have ever stood in — covering both parliamentary (_parlimen_) and state (_DUN_) elections, and spanning all general elections and by-elections in the database.

Results are returned as a JSON array, ordered from most recent to oldest. Each object represents one electoral contest and includes the seat, party, coalition, vote count, and outcome.
